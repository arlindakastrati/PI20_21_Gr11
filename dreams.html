Dreams

Dreaming of education is associated to your intelligence. You may dream of education in regards to yourself or even for someone around you.
To enroll in school indicates intelligence, trust, and know how. If the dream is of general education such as seeing a school or planning to save money for college, then the education itself is more abstract and usually refers to finances or work in your life. To talk to others about education means you will be thinking about how to improve yourself.

About

We are a group of six students who are trying to make a small difference in the world. Right now, we are studying Computer Engineering at the UP , Kosovo. We were asked to create a website as a project in our course called “The Internet” with programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and JS. Based on our experience from elementary and high school, we were not exposed to the programming world for different reasons.

One of them was the education system in our country. They did not include programming as part of the technology subject in school. But our love for technology made us study it. In the beginning, has been very hard for us, and During the last years we have witnessed the power that technology has to do many things, and changing the world is one of them.
